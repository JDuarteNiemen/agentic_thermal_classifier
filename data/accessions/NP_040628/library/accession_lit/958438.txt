Lambda repressor regulates the switch between PR and Prm promoters.

The DNA region containing the Or operator, Pr and Prm promoters and their transcription initiations
is sequenced. By binding to Or, repressor turns off Pr, turns on Prm and at higher concentrations
turns off Prm, regulating its own synthesis. Prm mRNA is unique in beginning immediately with the
initiation of translation, without a preceding leader sequence.
